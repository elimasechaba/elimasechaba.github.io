About Fuel Zone

A depot built on twenty years of showing up.

Fuel Zone started supplying fuel out of Welkom in the Free State in 2002. Ownership passed to Vassili Kyriakides in 2012, and the business grew from there into a nationwide bulk supplier, working as a distributor for major fuel brands across South Africa.

The client base reflects that consistency: farms, mines, factories, fuel retailers, stores and corporate fleets that all need the same thing — fuel that arrives at the volume and time it was promised.

Eli Masechaba’s role

As a fuel industry business specialist in association with Fuel Zone, Eli Masechaba is the point of contact for sales, quotes and orders — one direct relationship for clients who’d rather deal with a person than a call queue. Any fuel order placed through Eli is fulfilled by Fuel Zone’s own depot and delivery network.
